🏆 Star Performer

East Region Apparel generated $531K in H1 — 29% above target. Monthly growth accelerated from $69K in January to $105K in May. This is the blueprint for other regions.

⚠️ Watch Zone

West Region Electronics missed its monthly target in 5 of 6 months, accumulating a $87,000 shortfall. Potential causes: pricing, competition, or insufficient marketing spend.

Home & Garden grew +72% from Jan to Jun across all regions combined — driven by spring seasonal demand. Inventory and marketing for H2 should prioritize this category.
