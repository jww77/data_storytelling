📚 Central Finding

Attendance rate is the single strongest predictor of academic success. Students with ≥90% attendance average a GPA of 3.62, while those below 80% average just 2.71. Furthermore, students working part-time average 0.4 GPA points lower than their peers — an equity gap that warrants targeted intervention. Nursing consistently leads in GPA while Engineering shows the widest performance spread.
