📊 Strategic Insights & Optimization Opportunities

🏆 Double Down

Email campaigns deliver an average 900%+ ROI across all segments at minimal spend. This channel is chronically underbudgeted — every $1K shifted from Display to Email is estimated to generate $8K+ in additional revenue.

⚠️ Watch Zone

Display Ads show the lowest conversion rates in every segment (typically 0.1% vs 1–3% for other channels). The $55K+ spent on Display in Q4 alone could have been reallocated to generate an estimated $250K+ more revenue.

✂️ Reallocation Candidate

Q4 Holiday campaigns generated 38% of full-year Retail revenue with 3x better efficiency than Q1–Q2 combined. Shifting budget seasonally — reducing Q1–Q2 spend by 15% and increasing Q4 by 20% — projects a significant uplift.
